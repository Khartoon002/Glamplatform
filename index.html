What is Glamour and how does it work?

Glamour Platform is a European institution that partners with Africans to showcase their lifestyle, creativity, and daily habits — and get paid for it. It connects African expression to a global audience that values, engages, and pays in Euros and Pounds.

You earn by showcasing your lifestyle — your outfits, daily routines, cuisine, culture, and creative interests — to a global audience that pays you directly through the Glamour Platform in Euros and Pounds.
